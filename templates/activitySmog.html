INFERENCE

The following analysis was made by taking into account only thosee tweets that had been geotagged as this would be more fruitful

Although the number of tweets collected for the two incidents varied, it can be seen that a larger number of tweets concerning the Mumbai Rains were geotagged when compared to those concerning the smog problem in Delhi

IT can also be seen that there are more tweets originating from Mumbai regarding the rains. This could mean that the public see this as a problem to be handled by the citizens of Mumbai alone and do not see it as a national issue

The tweets regarding the problem of smog in Delhi on the other hand, seem to have different dynamics. There are more number of tweets from outside delhi when compared to those from inside. This could lead us to believe that Delhi, being the capital of the country, get more attention on social media.
